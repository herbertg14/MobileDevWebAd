University of Texas Services App

Whether you are a prospective student, current student, or just visiting the university, this app will make your UT experience easier and more enjoyable!

Everything you need in one place!

We know student life can be busy and stressful, so we've developed an app to bring everything you might need in one place! Our menu allows you to select services you might need without having to search for it on your own!

Canvas Access

Our app redirects you to Canvas giving you easy access to your classes and assignments.

Campus Maps

Find campus buildings from a list with the use of Google Maps.

Class Schedule

Find your classes by looking at your UT schedule.
